How to play ?

Press Z to go up !

Press s to go down !

Orange box : you're orange ? Hit orange cube !

Blue box : you're blue ? Hit blue cube !

Green box : you're green ? Hit green cube !

Highspeed : your speed is increased

Spike Cube : If you touch it, you die

Random Cube : Generate a random rule, get lucky !

Switch Cube : The rules are reversed, warning !

Counter Clock : You have more time to make a choice !

Reverse Cube : The game direction has shifted !
